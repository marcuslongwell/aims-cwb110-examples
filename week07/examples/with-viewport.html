The Viewport Meta Tag

Below is a bunch of lorem ipsum text (nonsense text for the sake of testing what your page will look like) and some images. On a full-screen browser, this text looks great. It's readable and takes and appropriate size on the screen.

However, if you open up the mobile developer tools in Firefox, you'll notice that the text looks incredibly small on this page. That's because this page doesn't have a meta viewport defined.

This is due to the pixel-density of your screen. In other words, on phones and mobile devices, pixels are a lot smaller compared to your laptop screen. So when you specify pixels for your font sizes and your image sizes, the pixels on a phone that display the text/images are also a lot smaller. We can rectify this with a meta tag!
